Games

Simple implementations of a variety of classic games and some fun (well, maybe) variations.

backgammon

The most ancient game.

chess

A war of attrition.

Variations:

  • Jetan (Edgar Rice Burroughs' Martian chess)
  • 3D Chess

dots

Also known as pigpen, dots is a two-player pencil-and-paper game.

Variations:

  • 3D boxes

go

A game of conquest.

Variations:

  • Multiple board sizes
  • Othello
  • 3- or 4-player versions

sprouts

A pencil-and-paper game with "interesting mathematical properties". Similar to dots.

sudoku

Can you count to nine? How about 0xf? Board generator and solver for traditional 9x9 sudoku and a hexadecimal variation.
